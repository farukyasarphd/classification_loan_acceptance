Our business goal is to predict the loan buyers and it would be costly to the company to miss classify those clients who are likely to buy a loan. For this purpose, it is crucial to minimize false negatives, which can be identified by maximizing Recall.

Gradient Boosting and XGBoost models are the models with the highest Recall and the primary candidates for best performing models. On top of that, Gradient Boosting has slightly better Accuracy, Precision and F1 Score than XGBoost.

Random Forest is also a strong candidate, since it has the highest F1 Score and Precision. In this business case, false positives are not a high risk. But if they were, then Random Forest would be a better option with overall best performance.

The refined model has consistently high recall scores in the 5 folds of subsets with high mean and low std. This ensures the model performs with high efficiency and reliability.

Gradient Boosting (GB) after tuning for best paramters have these advantages over other models (initial and tuned XGBoost):

  1. Achieves a higher recall score than initial model, demonstrating successful optimization for minimizing False Negatives.
  2. Shows better alignment between training (cross-validation) and test performance. So it generalizes more effectively than XGBoost in this scenario.
  3. Strikes a balance between fitting and predictive power, making it the more reliable model for production.
